The first question isn't "which hoop?" — it's "how much do you want to notice you're wearing it?" A hoop you won't think about after the second cup of coffee is a hoop you'll wear for a decade. Three details get you there.

## Weight before diameter

Most guides start with diameter. We start with weight. A 30mm hoop in 18k gold weighs about 4g; the same hoop in hollow tube weighs 1.8g. Solid hoops sit — they don't bounce when you laugh or lean. Hollow hoops travel well and survive accidental tugs without denting. Pick the failure mode you'd rather live with.

## Diameter depends on your hair

A 20mm hoop disappears against a short bob and reads serious on a pixie. A 40mm hoop looks like an earring against a pixie and like a statement against braids. If you tie your hair up three days a week, err larger. If you never do, err smaller.

## Hinge feel is everything

The click — whether the post seats firmly into its groove — is the thing we spend the most bench time on. If you can't feel the hoop close, you won't trust it, and you'll stop wearing it. When you try a hoop, open and close it three times; if there's any play at all, send it back.
